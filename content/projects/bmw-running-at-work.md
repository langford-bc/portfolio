challenge: >
  BMW Canada wanted to showcase the human element behind their high-performance brand,
  focusing on internal culture and the concept of 'running at work' as a metaphor for
  efficiency and passion.
strategy: >
  Instead of focusing on the cars, we focused on the people. We captured the kinetic energy
  of the workplace, using cinematic, candid visuals to document the drive and dedication of
  the team.
outcome: >
  The campaign created a powerful internal narrative that resonated externally, humanizing
  the brand and reinforcing BMW's reputation as a company driven by passionate individuals.
